Affordable & High Quality

All our jewellery is made to order, that means our investment in inventory is low so our costs are low. Unlike other jewellers, we manufacture our own jewellery and source the raw material directly, cutting all middlemen, thus ensuring lowest material cost also. This benefit we pass on to our customers thus offering the best product at the most reasonable prices. All our products go through 7-step quality check procedure before being shipped out.
